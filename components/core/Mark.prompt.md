The Bloqr logo — `Mark` is the three descending bars (white, cyan, orange); `Logo` adds the wordmark + sub-label. The cyan middle bar is a fixed brand element and must always appear.

```jsx
<Mark size={28} />
<Logo size={24} />
<Logo size={20} sub={null} />
```

- Never recolor, rotate, or reorder the bars. Orange is always the shortest, white the longest.
- Minimum mark height 24px. On dark backgrounds (the only Bloqr surface) use as-is.
